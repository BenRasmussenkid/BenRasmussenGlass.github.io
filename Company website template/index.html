Artist Bio:

Ben Rasmussen is a 14 year old glass artist that started creating glass at age 13.
He began learning about glass art by mentoring with Black Sand Glass owner Joel Corley.
Ben also participated in a class series with Milon Townsend.
Ben is a creative young entrepreneur, who has been selling his various creations since her was seven years old. Ben now sells his glass art to rent torch time from Black Sands Glass, and aspires to have a glass shop of his own. Ben is currently accepting donations for the construction of his glass studio through .
Ben started an apprenticeship with Daniel Moe, owner of Moe Hot Glass. The apprenticeship offers hands-on experience making and assisting in making soft glass art. During this sessions of the apprenticeship Ben has made several glasses, a vase, decorative plates and cups, and paperweights.
As Ben Rasmussen continues to expand his knowledge and experience working with glass art, his passion, creativity, and interest for creating glass art only increases.
Ben spends his free time when he isn't behind a torch working on coding and website development. Ben enjoys being outside with nature, expanding his knowledge and creativity, and spending quality time with his family and friends.
